Welcome to:
Pole Position Karts


Welcome to Pole Position Karts, Zimbabwe’s premier karting destination. Located in the heart of Harare, our state-of-the-art, outdoor karting circuit offers an unforgettable experience for karting enthusiasts of all levels.

Pole Position Karts provides an exciting, action-packed adventure with top-notch facilities. Whether you’re coming for a fun day out with friends, a corporate event, or a team-building session, we cater to all groups, large or small.

From sharpening your driving skills to enjoying a friendly race, Pole Position Karts promises a thrilling and memorable experience. Perfect for corporate events, birthday parties, or just a fun outing, let us make your time with us exceptional!

Ultimate Fun - Active Partnership

Ultimate Fun Zimbabwe is the ultimate family recreational sports centre located at the Hellenic Sports Club in Eastlea, offering a world-class, non-threatening, and social environment for fun, adventure, and fitness. Whether you're a sports enthusiast, a beginner, or just looking for a fun day out with the family, we’ve got you covered! We specialize in providing an exciting and safe venue where you and your family can try out a variety of thrilling activities including go-karting, trampoline gymnastics, putt-putt, and paintball. Our diverse selection of sports offers something for everyone, regardless of age or skill level. At Ultimate Fun, we believe that sports should be fun first and foremost. Our activities are designed to introduce you to the joy of sports in a laid-back and social environment, giving you the chance to learn new skills while enjoying some friendly competition. From go-karting with guidance from experienced marshals and the expertise of Zimbabwe's own karting champion, Zac Dufty, to the light-hearted but challenging world of putt-putt, there’s plenty to keep you entertained.

For those seeking to enhance their fitness levels and explore something different, trampoline gymnastics offers the perfect mix of fitness, flexibility, and acrobatic fun. Our eight trampoline mats provide a unique opportunity for young athletes to learn new tricks and perfect their skills in a safe and supervised environment. But the excitement doesn’t stop there! Paintball Blitz, with two adrenaline-pumping fields—Urban and Woods—adds an exciting twist to team-based sports. Challenge your tactical skills, improve your accuracy, and experience the thrill of high-energy action as you work with your team to achieve victory! We are dedicated to providing a fun, safe, and welcoming environment where families can bond, laugh, and create unforgettable memories. With trained staff ensuring your safety and offering guidance every step of the way, Ultimate Fun is the perfect destination for a day of excitement, fitness, and quality time with those who matter most.

Join us at Ultimate Fun, where fun never ends!

ROK CUP VORTEX - Active Partnership

The ROK Series is the only place in South Africa where drivers can qualify for prestigious international karting events.

The ROK Cup South Africa is the premier karting series, offering unparalleled opportunities for drivers to qualify for prestigious international events. As the only series in the country providing a direct pathway to global competitions, ROK Cup South Africa allows drivers to compete for spots in top-tier international events, including the T4 Nations Cup, ROK Cup Superfinal, FIA Karting Academy Trophy, and the FIA OK-N World Cup. With our exclusive access to these elite motorsport platforms, ROK Cup South Africa is the logical choice for aspiring drivers looking to take their talent to the global stage.

At ROK Cup South Africa, we believe in maintaining the highest standards of fairness and integrity in every race. Our use of pool engines ensures a level playing field, giving every driver an equal opportunity to succeed based on skill and dedication, rather than equipment advantage. This approach fosters a competitive yet supportive environment where the focus is on developing raw talent and promoting sportsmanship, making it the ideal setting for both newcomers and experienced drivers alike.
