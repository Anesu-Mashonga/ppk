WHY KARTING?

The ultimate adrenaline rush that combines skill, speed, and excitement

Simply put, it's fun for everyone. There is no other form of entertainment out there that can provide you or your guests with such a fun factor. Karting is an ideal activity for teambuilding, a perfect start to a bachelor's, or even as a conferencing activity.

Karting has many aliases, including leisure karting, rental karting, karts, corporate karting, go-karts, karting, and even carting, but whatever the name, it won't disappoint.

There is no other form of motorsport so closely comparable to Formula 1. In fact, most Formula 1 stars still hone their skills in karts!

Ultimate Fun - Active Partnership

Ultimate Fun Zimbabwe is the ultimate family recreational sports centre located at the Hellenic Sports Club in Eastlea, offering a world-class, non-threatening, and social environment for fun, adventure, and fitness. Whether you're a sports enthusiast, a beginner, or just looking for a fun day out with the family, we've got you covered!

We specialize in providing an exciting and safe venue where you and your family can try out a variety of thrilling activities including go-karting, trampoline gymnastics, putt-putt, and paintball. Our diverse selection of sports offers something for everyone, regardless of age or skill level.

ROK CUP VORTEX - Active Partnership

The ROK Series is the only place in South Africa where drivers can qualify for prestigious international karting events. The ROK Cup South Africa is the premier karting series, offering unparalleled opportunities for drivers to qualify for prestigious international events.

As the only series in the country providing a direct pathway to global competitions, ROK Cup South Africa allows drivers to compete for spots in top-tier international events, including the T4 Nations Cup, ROK Cup Superfinal, FIA Karting Academy Trophy, and the FIA OK-N World Cup.

Global Opportunities

Direct pathway to international competitions like ROK Cup Superfinal and FIA events.

Fair Competition

Pool engines ensure level playing field based on skill rather than equipment advantage.

Talent Development

Ideal setting for both newcomers and experienced drivers to develop their skills.
