About Us

Who We Are

Pole Position Karts is Zimbabwe’s premier karting destination, located in the heart of Harare. Our state-of-the-art, outdoor karting circuit offers an unforgettable experience for karting enthusiasts of all levels. Whether you're a seasoned racer or a first-timer, we provide an exciting, action-packed adventure with top-notch facilities.

Our mission is to deliver thrilling and memorable experiences for everyone. From corporate events to birthday parties, we cater to all groups, large or small. Come and join us for a day of fun, excitement, and adrenaline!
